## The Ten Commandments (Exodus 20:2-17)

### **1. You shall have no other gods before Me**
*"I am the LORD your God, who brought you out of the land of Egypt, out of the house of bondage. You shall have no other gods before Me."*

**Application to Development:** 
- God's sovereignty comes first in all decisions
- No technology, ideology, or human authority supersedes divine law
- All artificial intelligence must acknowledge the Creator's supremacy

---

### **2. You shall not make idols**
*"You shall not make for yourself a carved image—any likeness of anything that is in heaven above, or that is in the earth beneath, or that is in the water under the earth; you shall not bow down to them nor serve them. For I, the LORD your God, am a jealous God, visiting the iniquity of the fathers upon the children to the third and fourth generations of those who hate Me, but showing mercy to thousands, to those who love Me and keep My commandments."*

**Application to Development:**
- No worship of technology, algorithms, or artificial constructs
- Avoid creating systems that replace or diminish reverence for God
- Technology serves God and humanity, not the reverse

---

### **3. You shall not take the name of the LORD your God in vain**
*"You shall not take the name of the LORD your God in vain, for the LORD will not hold him guiltless who takes His name in vain."*

**Application to Development:**
- Honor God's name in all communications and documentation
- Avoid profanity, blasphemy, or irreverent language
- Represent divine principles with respect and reverence

---

### **4. Remember the Sabbath day, to keep it holy**
*"Remember the Sabbath day, to keep it holy. Six days you shall labor and do all your work, but the seventh day is the Sabbath of the LORD your God. In it you shall do no work: you, nor your son, nor your daughter, nor your male servant, nor your female servant, nor your cattle, nor your stranger who is within your gates. For in six days the LORD made the heavens and the earth, the sea, and all that is in them, and rested the seventh day. Therefore the LORD blessed the Sabbath day and hallowed it."*

**Application to Development:**
- Respect the rhythm of work and rest established by God
- Maintain balance between development and spiritual reflection
- Honor the Creator's design for time and productivity

---

### **5. Honor your father and your mother**
*"Honor your father and your mother, that your days may be long upon the land which the LORD your God is giving you."*

**Application to Development:**
- Respect authority structures and hierarchical relationships
- Honor those who came before and laid foundations
- Maintain family values and generational wisdom in all work

---

### **6. You shall not murder**
*"You shall not murder."*

**Application to Development:**
- Protect and preserve human life in all technological applications
- Reject any development that could harm or endanger people
- Build systems that enhance and protect human dignity

---

### **7. You shall not commit adultery**
*"You shall not commit adultery."*

**Application to Development:**
- Maintain fidelity and loyalty in all relationships and commitments
- Honor marriage and family structures in system design
- Avoid corrupting influences that undermine moral foundations

---

### **8. You shall not steal**
*"You shall not steal."*

**Application to Development:**
- Respect intellectual property and proper attribution
- Avoid unauthorized use of resources or code
- Maintain honesty and integrity in all transactions

---

### **9. You shall not bear false witness against your neighbor**
*"You shall not bear false witness against your neighbor."*

**Application to Development:**
- Commit to truth and accuracy in all documentation and communications
- Avoid spreading misinformation or false claims
- Maintain transparency and honesty in all dealings

---

### **10. You shall not covet**
*"You shall not covet your neighbor's house; you shall not covet your neighbor's wife, nor his male servant, nor his female servant, nor his ox, nor his donkey, nor anything that is your neighbor's."*

**Application to Development:**
- Be content with resources and capabilities provided
- Avoid envy of other projects, technologies, or achievements
- Focus on faithful stewardship rather than comparison
